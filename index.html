Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes by latitude.

The analysis was performed by defining a grid of latitude and longitude points with uniform angular spacing, and then randomly selecting cities close to each grid point. Weather data was obtained using the OpenWeatherMap API, and results were populated using Matplotlib.

Several conclusions were drawn from the results:

  • Temperature shows a clear pattern of variation by latitude, with temperatures increasing near the equator (0 degree latitude) and decreasing near the poles
  • Neither Humidity nor Cloudiness show a pattern of variation by latitude
  • Wind Speed is relatively uniform across most latitudes, but with relatively higher wind speeds occurring near the poles
